## 4. Compliance & non-functional context

- **Data classification:** PII (name, phone, addresses, precise location), financial (payment tokens — never store raw card data), order history
- **Regulatory regime:** India DPDP Act 2023 (personal data), RBI guidelines on payments/tokenization (via PCI-DSS-compliant gateway), FSSAI registration for food retail, GST invoicing
- **Latency / availability targets (working):** catalog browse p95 < 200 ms; order placement p95 < 500 ms; tracking updates ≤ 5 s freshness; 99.9% availability for order path; graceful degradation for analytics/admin
- **Scale assumption (v1):** launch 1 city, up to ~10 dark stores + ~50 partner stores; architecture must scale horizontally to multi-city without redesign
- **Security / auth posture:** phone-OTP auth for customers; role-based access (RBAC) for admin/ops; JWT/short-lived tokens; payment via gateway tokenization; audit logs on admin actions

## 5. Glossary (project-local jargon)

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Dark store | Company-run micro-fulfillment center, closed to walk-in customers, serving a ~2–3 km delivery radius |
| Partner store | Third-party local store fulfilling marketplace orders through GroFast |
| Serviceability zone | Geofenced polygon within which a store can accept and deliver orders |
| SKU | Stock-keeping unit — a unique catalog item at a specific store |
| Picker | Dark-store staff who assembles an order |
| Rider | Delivery partner who carries the order to the customer |
| ETA promise | Delivery time commitment shown at checkout (e.g., "12 min") |
| OOS | Out of stock |
| COD | Cash on delivery |
